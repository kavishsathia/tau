Help

When querying, use this format:

{query} -t {topic} -y {year}

When uploading, use this format:

@qn@{title},{year},{topic}@qn@
{rawLatex}
@qn@{title},{year},{topic}@qn@
{rawLatex}

Tau has tried to make errors a first-class citizen,
so if you have a flaw in your rawLatex, you will be able
to see a log of the errors in your contribution page.
